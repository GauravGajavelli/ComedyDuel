# S02E08 — "The Heart Attack" — adult cookie freedom / appetite ruining

You know, I tell ya, I gotta say that I'm enjoying adulthood.
For a lot of reasons.
And, I'll tell you reason number one: as an adult, if I want a cookie, I have a cookie, okay?
I have three cookies or four cookies, or eleven cookies if I want.

Many times I will intentionally ruin my entire appetite.
Just ruin it.

And then, I call my mother up right after to tell her that I did it.
"Hello, Mom? yeah, I just ruined my entire appetite.. cookies."

So what if you ruin..
See, because as an adult, we understand even if you ruin an appetite, there's another appetite coming right behind it.
There's no danger in running out of appetites.
I've got millions of them, I'll ruin them whenever I want!
